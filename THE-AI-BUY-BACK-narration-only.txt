THE AI BUY-BACK — NARRATION SCRIPT
2:46 Video | Record in One Take or Section-by-Section

TONE: Professional, urgent, research-backed. High-energy but authoritative.
Think: Scott Magnacca voice—confident, fast-paced, compelling.

============================================================
SECTION 1: THE HOOK (0:00 - 0:30) — 30 Seconds
============================================================

[Start with energy. Slow first line for emphasis.]

History doesn't just happen—it divides.

Just like electricity in 1880, or the internet in 1990, we've hit a
"before and after" line. And it's happening right now.

You're not looking at another tool. You're looking at your chance to
transition from an operator—drowning in endless tasks—to an owner.
Someone who sets the strategy and lets systems execute.

The question isn't whether AI will change your business.
The question is: will you lead that change, or manage it?


============================================================
SECTION 2: THE PROMISE (0:30 - 1:15) — 45 Seconds
============================================================

[Energy increases. Faster delivery, forward momentum.]

The mission is crystal clear: buy back your time.

Not marginal gains. Not "a few hours here and there."

We're talking about reclaiming 15 hours of your week.

AI isn't coming for your job. The person using AI is coming for yours.

[Slight pause—let it land.]

The real gap isn't technical. It's psychological. It's the
"AI Confidence Gap"—the space between knowing you should use AI
and actually installing it into your workflow.

Today, we're closing that gap. From confusion to certainty.
From waiting to winning.


============================================================
SECTION 3: THE FRAMEWORK — THE FOUR LEVERS (1:15 - 2:15) — 60 Seconds
============================================================

[Music shifts. Staccato delivery. One lever per beat.]

Success isn't magic. It's mechanics. Four levers. Master them,
and you own the space.

---

LEVER ONE: ACTIVATE.

Train AI as your second brain. It needs to know your tone.
Your Storyselling methodology. Your specific constraints.
Your voice can't be lost to automation.

An untrained AI is like an untrained employee.
Expensive. Ineffective. Dangerous.

---

LEVER TWO: ACCELERATE.

Blank page syndrome kills more great ideas than bad ideas ever could.
AI removes the friction of starting. You move from "I have a concept"
to "here's the strategic outline" in 90 seconds.

Instead of staring at a blank screen for 2 hours,
you're refining a framework in 10 minutes.

---

LEVER THREE: AMPLIFY.

One core idea. Fifty authentic outputs.
A single masterpiece becomes a movement.

Traditional content creation: create, publish, done.
AI amplification: create, adapt, distribute across every channel—
without losing your voice.

This is where leverage lives.

---

LEVER FOUR: AUGMENT.

You're no longer a team of one.
You're leading a multidisciplinary war room.

Research that used to take your team three days? Done in 30 minutes.
Analysis that required hiring a consultant? In-house, on-demand.

You scale without hiring. You level up without burning out.


============================================================
SECTION 4: THE PSYCHOLOGY & CTA (2:15 - 2:46) — 31 Seconds
============================================================

[Music drops. Intimate, conversational. Slower delivery. Direct eye contact.]

Here's what separates the owners from the operators:
psychology, not mechanics.

You can know all four levers. You can understand the framework perfectly.
But if you don't move, if you stay in "learning mode," the person
next to you—the one who actually implements—they'll own the market.

Psychology beats mechanics every time.

Don't give yourself years to adapt. Give yourself months.
Massive leverage comes from urgency.

You've seen the map. You know the four levers.
Now you need to know where YOU stand.

Take the 60-Second AI Quiz right now.

Find your gaps. Claim your 15 hours.
And let's get back to the work that actually matters.

I'll see you on the other side.

============================================================
END OF NARRATION
