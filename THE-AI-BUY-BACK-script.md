## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Audio Mixing
- **Narration:** 0.75 volume (clear, punchy)
- **Background Music:** 0.15 volume (supportive, not intrusive)
- **Ambience/SFX:** Subtle (cash register ding at "15 hours," whoosh transitions, tech beeps at lever reveals)

### B-Roll Requirements
- Gas lamp → lightbulb sequence (stock footage, 1880s aesthetic)
- Dial-up modem visual (retro, sepia)
- Fiber optic cable (cyan lighting)
- Modern professional at desk (exhausted facial expression)
- Person closing laptop, walking to window (natural light)
- Conference room scene (high-stakes, strategic)
- Family dinner (warmth, presence)
- Team whiteboard session (collaboration)
- Direct-to-camera presenter (professional, confident, 30 seconds)

### Graphics Build
- **Split Screen:** Operator vs. Owner (gold dividing line, animated text builds)
- **Digital Clock:** Counting down from 08:00 to 15:00:00 (glowing, futuristic font)
- **Four Quadrants:** Grid layout, each lighting up sequentially with icons and descriptions
- **Text Overlays:** All gold (#f5a623), minimum 65px for hero text
- **QR Code:** Glowing cyan, 200×200px minimum, high contrast against background

### Animation Pacing
- **0:00-0:30:** Fast cuts (3 fps), high energy
- **0:30-1:15:** Medium pace (moderate b-roll), building momentum
- **1:15-2:15:** Synchronized with dialogue (lever reveals on beat drops)
- **2:15-2:46:** Slow, intentional (presenter delivers CTA with weight)

### Timestamp Verification (Whisper)
- Hook ends at ~30s mark
- Promise ends at ~45s mark
- Levers 1-4 span 60s total (45s → 105s)
- CTA begins at ~105s, ends at ~166s
