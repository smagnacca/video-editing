# LinkedIn DM Templates

> Use Scott's voice: direct, credentialed, contrarian, never salesy. Reference their world specifically.
> **Personalization variables:** `{name}`, `{company}`, `{role}`, `{specific_post_topic}`, `{shared_connection}`

---

## TEMPLATE A — Connection Request (Cold, Targeted ICP)

```
Hi {name} — saw your post on {specific_post_topic}. The point about {one_specific_idea} matched
something I was teaching at Babson last week. Would be glad to connect — no pitch, just curious
how {company} is thinking about it.
```

**Why it works:** Specific (proves you read), credentialed (Babson), explicitly no-pitch.
**Acceptance rate target:** 35%+

---

## TEMPLATE B — First DM After Connection Accepted

```
Thanks for connecting, {name}. I just shipped a 90-sec breakdown of the one mistake I see most
{role}s making with AI right now — costs them about 56% of the wage premium they could be earning.
[link]

Worth 90 seconds — happy to hear what you'd push back on.
```

**Why it works:** Gives value first, frames as conversation not pitch, low-friction CTA.
**CTA:** Latest TOFU video

---

## TEMPLATE C — Quiz Drop (Soft TOFU)

```
{name} — quick one. Built a 60-second AI Skills quiz that benchmarks where {role}s actually stand
vs. 12,000+ peers. Free, no signup wall.

Curious where you'd land: 60-second-ai-quiz.netlify.app
```

**Why it works:** Specific number (12,000), no-friction promise (no signup), curiosity hook.
**CTA:** 60-second quiz

---

## TEMPLATE D — Sprint Invite (After Quiz Engagement)

```
Saw you took the quiz — nice work landing in the {tier}.

I'm running a free 15-min Sprint on {date} where I'll walk through the exact playbook the top 10%
of {role}s use to {outcome}. One move, one page, deploy Monday.

Want me to save you a seat? 15-minute-sales-sprint.netlify.app
```

**Why it works:** Acknowledges their action, specific time commitment, "one move" framing.
**CTA:** 15-min Sprint

---

## TEMPLATE E — Workshop Upgrade (After Sprint Attended)

```
{name} — appreciated you joining the Sprint. The thing you mentioned about {their_q_or_comment}
is exactly what we go deep on in the AI Mastery Summit (June 18, free, virtual).

3 days, 90 min each, CFO-validated ROI frameworks. VIP seat is $49 if you want recordings + Q&A.

the-ai-mastery-summit.netlify.app — want me to add you?
```

**Why it works:** References their specific contribution, dual offer (free + paid VIP).
**CTA:** AI Mastery Summit

---

## TEMPLATE F — Re-Engage Dormant Lead (60+ days quiet)

```
{name} — random thought. We connected after my {original_topic} post a couple months back. I just
released a new piece on {recent_video_topic} that I think hits closer to what you were asking about.

Worth a look? [link]

Either way — hope {company}'s having a good run.
```

**Why it works:** Acknowledges silence honestly, low-pressure check-in, tied to their interest.

---

## TEMPLATE G — Direct Course Pitch (Hot Lead, Score 4-5)

```
{name} — based on where you're sitting in the funnel and what you've shared, I think the
4-Day AI Sales Catalyst is the right fit. $249, self-paced, you'd be done by Friday.

The whole thing maps to the wage-premium framework you mentioned in {recent_msg}.

4daycourse.netlify.app — happy to answer any questions before you decide.
```

**Why it works:** Direct, references their specific signals, low-priced entry, easy out.
**CTA:** 4-Day Course

---

## What NOT to Send

- ❌ "Hope you're doing well" openers
- ❌ "I'd love to jump on a quick call to learn about your business"
- ❌ Anything with "synergy", "leverage", "circle back"
- ❌ Pasting a CTA without a personalized line above it
- ❌ Sending the same template to a connection 2x within 14 days
