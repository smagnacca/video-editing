## Quick Reference

- **Score 5** = Hot — bought or about to buy. Personal touch only.
- **Score 4** = Warm — engaged twice, ready for sprint/workshop CTA.
- **Score 3** = Lukewarm — opened email, viewed video, but no reply yet.
- **Score 2** = Cold-but-fit — right ICP, no engagement signal.
- **Score 1** = Discovery — added from list, not yet contacted.
